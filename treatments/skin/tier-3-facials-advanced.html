advanced skin needling

Skin needling for collagen stimulation, tightening and rejuvenation

Skin needling penetrates the skin and causes controlled damage to the surface and sub-dermal layers. This increases collagen and fibroblast production which assists with scars, pores, acne, blackheads, pigmentation and wrinkles. Although kits and dermal rollers can be purchased for home use they simply do not compare to salon quality equipment that is able to penetrate the skin thousands of time per minute.
