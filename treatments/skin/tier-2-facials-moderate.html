Deep sea peel

$170

1 hour

*This is a highly stimulating peel and is not recommended for sensitive skin*

This is a natural peel that is massaged directly into the skin, but natural does not mean tame!

As it is rubbed into the skin crushed coral spores are embedded into the upper dermis and cannot be removed. The body recognises these as foreign objects and the only way to remove them is for the skin to shed itself. This shedding provides deep exfoliating for dramatically softer, rejuvenated and more even looking skin.

Includes:

  • A Skin Care Consultation

  • A Deep Cleanse

  • A High Performance Mask

  • Stimulating Deep Sea Peel

  • Speciality Eye Cream and Antioxidant Serum

  • SPF Sunscreen if required

  • A Complimentary Beer or Wine
